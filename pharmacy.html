Pharmacy

Join our Hope Ambassadors

Adding beats everyday

Nahdi is the largest retail pharmacy chain in the Middle East and North Africa (MENA) with over 1100+ stores around KSA and UAE. We have been the community's health and wellbeing partner for over 35 years. Our expert Pharmacists have been adding beats to the lives of our Guests every day ever since, and this is what has been driving us forward as a health pioneer.

Our Hope Ambassadors role as Community Pharmacists goes beyond filling prescriptions-- they provide our Guests with unmatched-quality care, guidance, and make a positive different in their lives by helping them take charge of their health in their wellbeing journey. To continue providing the best quality care for our Guests, our Pharmacists are in an on-going journey of professional and personal development that includes different training programs to continue adding beats to our Guests everyday.

Our investment in people sets us apart

Training Hours

Nahdi grows when our Pharmacists grow, our Hope Ambassadors go through more than 550+ training hours to build up their knowledge and expertise to add beats to our Guests, they’re trained throughout their career on more than 40+ different topics

Our Omnihealth Ecosystem

There are endless opportunities for our Pharmacists to grow their expertise in Nahdi’s one of a kind Omnihealth Ecosystem. Our Pharmacists are trained to be experts in both medication management, patient consultation, and Omnihealth topics, all the while helping our Guests get the best care they need

Career Development

Our Career Development programs offers our Pharmacists opportunities to develop personally and professionally, so they can not only excel in their current role but pave their own path for a career journey that beats with success
